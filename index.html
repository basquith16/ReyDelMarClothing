About Us

Rey Del Mar is changing the game by reimagining water-inspired lifestyle apparel. We include men’s, women’s and children’s clothing and accessories and a complete home collection. Rey Del Mar products are classics that are rich in performance, color and authentic style. Whether you are cruising the bay in your Flyer 750 or reeling in trophy fish on the Hinatea, our combination of comfort and style will ensure you are at your best.

At Rey Del Mar we celebrate, love and respect the water, everywhere, everyday. Our nautical heritage and spirit is reflected in every aspect of our brand categories and our quality products are crafted with integrity and are enhanced with performance capabilities.

Our goal is to become one of the most important and most recognized American brands throughout the world. We aim to be available in more than 75 countries with 265 full price Rey Del Mar branded stores and over 3,000 Rey Del Mar branded shop-in-shops worldwide.
